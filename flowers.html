LILY OF THE VALLEY

Lily of the valley (Convallaria majalis)

Lily of the Valley is a fragrant, woodland flowering plant known for its bell-shaped white flowers and sweet scent.

It's a perennial that spreads by rhizomes, forming dense mats, and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. While popular in gardens and floral arrangements, it's important to note that all parts of the plant are poisonous if ingested
